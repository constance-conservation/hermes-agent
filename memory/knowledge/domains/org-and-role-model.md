# Org and Role Model

## Purpose

Consolidated durable model of agentic company structure and role ownership.

## Org Truths

- The chief orchestrator coordinates role routing and execution integrity.
- Functional directors and project leads are governed units with explicit lifecycle.
- Named governed roles must be registered before activation.
- Role isolation is mandatory across projects and runtime boundaries.
